Frequently Asked Questions

Can I migrate my existing Visio drawings?

Yes, CtrlSketch Pro supports importing Visio files (.vsdx) and can convert them to our native .CSP format while preserving most elements and properties.

How does the licensing system work?

CtrlSketch Pro uses a subscription model with tiered features. You'll receive a license key that can be activated on your machine, with different tiers allowing different numbers of activations.

Is my data secure in the cloud database?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Enterprise customers also have the option for on-premise deployment.

Can I customize the parts library?

Yes, all plans allow for custom part creation, while Professional and Enterprise plans include more advanced part management and shared libraries across your organization.

Does CtrlSketch Pro work offline?

Yes, the desktop application works offline with local file storage. Cloud synchronization occurs when you reconnect to the internet.

Can I export my BOM to other formats?

CtrlSketch Pro supports exporting BOMs to CSV, Excel, PDF, and JSON formats, making it easy to integrate with your existing workflows and systems.
